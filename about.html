What is
ToolCraft?

ToolCraft is an independent, free-to-use utility platform housing 48 browser-based tools — from password generators and unit converters to CSS gradient builders, JWT decoders, and financial calculators.

Every single tool runs entirely within your browser. Nothing you type is ever transmitted to our servers. No accounts. No paywalls. No subscriptions. Just tools that work.

We're sustained by unobtrusive advertising so we can keep everything free, forever.
