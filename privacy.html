Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: ToolCraft collects no personal data. Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you type, calculate, or generate is ever sent to our servers.

1. Who We Are

ToolCraft (toolcraft.online) is a free, browser-based utility platform built and maintained by a small independent development collective. We provide free tools for everyday tasks — all processing happens client-side on your device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be crystal clear about this. ToolCraft does not collect, store, transmit, or process:

  • Any text you enter into our tools
  • Any files you use with our tools
  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Your IP address (beyond what Cloudflare logs for security)
  • Passwords you generate on our platform
  • Any form inputs, calculations, or results

Every calculation, conversion, generation, and transformation happens exclusively within your own browser. When you close the tab, nothing is retained.

3. Cookies & Advertising

ToolCraft uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may place cookies on your device to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We have no access to or control over cookies placed by Google AdSense. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

4. Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (page views, country of origin, device type). This analytics tool is privacy-first and does not use cookies or track individuals. No personally identifiable data is collected through this system.

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and security (may log IP addresses for DDoS protection)
  • Google AdSense — advertisement delivery
  • Google Fonts — typography (font files loaded from Google's CDN)
  • api.qrserver.com — QR code image generation (your QR input text is sent to this service only when you use the QR tool)

6. Children's Privacy

ToolCraft does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our tools are general-purpose utilities with no age-restricted content.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of ToolCraft after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
